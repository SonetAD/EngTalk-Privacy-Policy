Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 21, 2025

Thank you for using EngTalk ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following data when you use our App:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, gender, and profile photo (if uploaded), especially when you sign in through Google or other platforms (only if you authorize it).
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: Time spent in the app, features accessed, crash logs, and diagnostics.
  • Location Data: If you grant permission, we may collect approximate or precise location data. This is used to help match users by region and improve communication quality.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our features
  • Match users effectively for English conversation practice
  • Understand and analyze usage trends
  • Communicate with you regarding updates or support
  • Ensure safety, security, and compliance with legal obligations

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service providers and analytics tools (e.g., Google Firebase, Google Analytics)
  • Ad services (e.g., Google AdMob) to display ads within the App
  • Legal authorities, if required by law
  • Other parties, only with your explicit consent

4. Google OAuth and Third-Party Sign-In

If you choose to sign in using Google or another third-party provider, we may collect basic profile information such as name, email, and profile photo, only after your authorization.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for optional data use at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email below.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by applicable laws.

8. Children's Privacy

EngTalk is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have mistakenly collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. Third-Party Services

The App may contain links or integrate with third-party services such as Google, Firebase, or AdMob. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these services. Please review their policies independently.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Effective Date” and notify users through the App or our official website.

11. Data Deletion Without Account Removal

At EngTalk, we value your control over your data. We provide options to delete specific content without requiring complete account removal.

  • Voice Bites: Each voice bite you upload appears as a card within your profile or on the explore page. To delete a voice bite, simply tap the trash icon located on the card. This will immediately and permanently remove the recording from our servers.
  • Messages: To delete a chat message, long-press the message bubble. A delete option will appear, allowing you to remove that message from our servers permanently.

These in-app deletion tools are designed to offer flexibility while ensuring your content can be removed quickly and securely without affecting your overall account.
